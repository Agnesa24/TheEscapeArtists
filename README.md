## Project Description

### Game Genre
2D Escape Game ñ Action Adventure

### Game Idea
Our game is a 2D escape game with action-adventure elements. The player must explore, solve puzzles, and avoid enemies such as police officers and fellow inmates, in order to escape from a prison while uncovering the story behind their false imprisonment.

This game focuses on exploration, environmental interaction, and problem-solving rather than combat. The main challenge comes from navigating the prison, using objects wisely, and avoiding danger.

### Characters and Interaction
At the start of the game, the player chooses one of two playable characters:

- Lia (female twin)
- Enzo (male twin)

Both of these characters have the same gameplay mechanics but experience the story seperatey, as they are sent to different prisons.

### Enemies
- Prison guards who patrol areas and watch for suspicious behaviour
- Hostile inmates who may block paths or cause trouble

## Story
The game starts off with two characters, twin brother and sisters (you are able to pick who you would want to play as). If you choose to play as a girl, you would play as Lia, if you choose a to play as a boy, you would play as Enzo. The twins weren't criminals. They were working nights at a shipping yard by the docks. It was a quiet job. Good pay. No questions asked. Until one night, everything went wrong.

During a late shift, Enzo and Lia walked into the wrong warehouse at the wrong time. Inside, they saw a deal happening. Weapons, cash, people they recognized from the local city news. Before they could leave, the alarm went off. Someone fired a shot. Chaos broke out.

By the time the police arrived, the people who mattered were gone. The twins were the only ones left. The investigators found their fingerprints on the crates. A gun near their feet. The security footage cut out right before the deal. No witnesses stepped forward. The case was closed shut fast.

They tried to explain that they were jut there at the wrong place, at the wrong time. But they were accused of lying. The twins were each sent to separate sister prisons named StoneBridge Prison, one for women and one for men.

Inside the prison, word spreads fast. Some inmates think they are hiding something. Others think they took the fall for someone else. Guards watch them closer than the rest. Bu the twins know the truth... Someone out there wanted them locked up. And the prison is not where the story ends, it's where it begins...

## Gameplay Progression
As the game progresses and the levels become harder, the character will change. This makes the game more challenging and forces the player to adapt to new situations.

The game begins inside the prison, where Enzo wakes up in his cell. From there, the player explores the prison step by step. Each level represents a different part of the prison, such as the cell block, corridors, storage rooms, and guarded areas.

To move to the next level, the player must solve puzzles, find useful items, and choose safe paths. The character interacts with the environment by opening doors, hiding behind objects, using tools, and activating switches.

Enemies like prison guards and hostile inmates cannot be fought directly. The player must avoid them, sneak past them, or distract them in order to stay safe.
